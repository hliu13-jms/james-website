James's Website

Welcome to my personal website. I am currently studying Electronic Production and Design (EPD), where I focus on experimental sound creation. I spend a lot of my time patching my Buchla 208c modular synthesizer and programming custom tools in Max/MSP to generate unpredictable, harsh noise and intricate soundscapes. I really enjoy the physical feeling of patching cables and the raw analog sound it produces.
